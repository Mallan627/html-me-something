My Titanic Adventure

A trip to the Titanic museum in Branson, Missouri.

Back in December of 2009, my family and I went on a trip to Branson, MO. Among other things we visited was the Titanic museum. It was definitely one of the highlights of the trip!

The outside of the museum was made up to look like the actual Titanic (at least the front anyway) and they even had a little waterfall spraying up near what would have been the bow of the ship, making look as the ship plowed full steam ahead.
